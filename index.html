About

formhub is a free hosted service that makes data collection simple and collaborative.

Simple. All you need to get started is an account on formhub and the ability to author .xls files. Absolutely no technical setup required. Collaborative. Share your data collection forms in Excel. Find, grab and modify other peoples' forms and quickly deploy it. Publish gathered data to share it with others via Excel, CSV, KML or an interactive map. Mobile. formhub works seemlessly with ODK Collect a robust and proven data collection platform for Android.
