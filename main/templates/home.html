Welcome to formhub!  The hub for your mobile data collection needs

formhub is a free hosted service that aims to make the whole data collection process simpler and more collaborative.

formhub makes data collection simpler by making survey authoring simple, and by providing a hosted site in which going from authored survey to data is quick and painless (no technical understanding required). formhub works seemlessly with ODK Collect a robust and proven data collection platform for Android.

formhub makes data collection more collaborative by making it possible to author surveys/forms in Excel. Being both human-accessible and machine-readable makes XLSforms the ideal format for sharing forms that you quickly want to deploy.
