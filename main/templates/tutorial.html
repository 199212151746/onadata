Introduction

ODK Deploy aims to make collecting survey data with Android smart phones easy and free.

One uses ODK Deploy as follows:

  1. Translate survey into the xsl2xform syntax.
  2. Upload xls2xform file to ODK Deploy.
  3. Use ODK Collect to download survey and submit data.
  4. Use ODK Deploy to download and map survey data.
This tutorial will walk you through each stop of the way.

Translate survey into the xsl2xform syntax

The first step in collecting survey data is to actually write the survey. For this tutorial we will be using the following survey:

  1. What is your name?
  2. How old are you?
  3. May I take your picture?
  4. Do you have any children?
  5. Record your GPS coordinates.
  6. What web browsers do you use?
The second step is writing this survey in the xls2xform syntax. The idea behind this approach is that each question in the survey is a row in the spreadsheet. By using a spreadsheet we can expose most of the XForm features supported by ODK Collect. Check out the images to the right that show the xls2xform syntax for this survey.



Upload xls2xform file to ODK Deploy

After creating an account and signing into the site you can publish surveys for use with ODK Collect. On the right, we submit tutorial.xls, described above, for publication.

After publishing our first survey, a new table appears showing our new survey. Next we need to use ODK Collect to submit data for this survey...

Use ODK Collect to download survey and submit data

The url of this web application must be given to ODK Collect before it will get forms from and submit data to ODK Deploy. In Collect's Main Menu, press the Menu button. Select Server Preferences, then Server. Enter http://odk-deploy.org/your-username as the server.

Download your published form to your phone.

Fill out the survey on your phone.

After filling out the survey on your phone submit the data to the server.

Use ODK Deploy to download and map survey data

After submitting data from ODK Collect to the server you can now download the data as a nicely formatted csv or xls file.
